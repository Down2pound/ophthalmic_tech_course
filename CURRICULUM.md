## Daily Modules

### Day 1: Ophthalmic Fundamentals & Anatomy
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Basic eye anatomy overview
  - Visual pathway and function
  - Common terminology and abbreviations
  - Refractive errors fundamentals
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Understand basic ocular anatomy
  - Recognize common eye conditions
  - Fluency in ophthalmic terminology
- **Assessment:** Anatomy quiz

### Day 2: Patient Assessment & History Taking
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Chief complaint documentation
  - Comprehensive eye history
  - Visual acuity assessment
  - Patient communication techniques
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Conduct thorough patient interviews
  - Document findings accurately
  - Demonstrate professional communication
- **Assessment:** Mock patient consultation

### Day 3: Diagnostic Equipment - Part 1 (Refraction)
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Automated refraction
  - Manual refraction techniques
  - Phoropter operation
  - Lens selection and verification
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Operate refraction equipment confidently
  - Assist with refractive procedures
  - Troubleshoot common equipment issues
- **Assessment:** Hands-on refraction practicum

### Day 4: Diagnostic Equipment - Part 2 (Imaging)
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Optical coherence tomography (OCT)
  - Fundus photography
  - Visual fields testing
  - Corneal topography
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Capture quality diagnostic images
  - Understand imaging principles
  - Maintain and calibrate equipment
- **Assessment:** Image quality evaluation

### Day 5: Clinical Procedures & Measurements
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Intraocular pressure (IOP) measurement
  - Gonioscopy principles
  - Biometry and A-scan
  - Specular microscopy
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Perform precise clinical measurements
  - Prepare patients for procedures
  - Quality control and accuracy standards
- **Assessment:** Measurement competency check

### Day 6: Anterior Segment Examination
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Slit-lamp biomicroscopy
  - Anterior chamber evaluation
  - Corneal and lens assessment
  - Lid and lash examination
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Perform detailed anterior segment exams
  - Identify pathology and abnormalities
  - Document findings appropriately
- **Assessment:** Anterior segment case studies

### Day 7: Posterior Segment Examination
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Indirect ophthalmoscopy
  - Direct ophthalmoscopy
  - Dilated fundus examination
  - Retinal pathology recognition
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Perform fundus examinations
  - Recognize retinal conditions
  - Assist with posterior segment procedures
- **Assessment:** Posterior segment findings interpretation

### Day 8: Surgical Assistance & Instrumentation
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Surgical instruments and setup
  - OR protocols and safety
  - Cataract surgery assistance
  - Refractive surgery overview
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Support surgical procedures confidently
  - Maintain sterile technique
  - Understand surgical workflow
- **Assessment:** Surgical protocol exam

### Day 9: Practice Management & Patient Communication
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Patient relations and retention
  - Insurance and billing basics
  - EMR systems and documentation
  - Patient education techniques
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Enhance patient experience
  - Handle administrative tasks
  - Communicate effectively with all staff
- **Assessment:** Role-play scenarios

### Day 10: Advanced Topics & Certification Preparation
- **Duration:** Full day
- **Topics:**
  - Specialty lens fitting
  - Contact lens basics
  - Laser procedures overview
  - Comprehensive review and Q&A
  - Certification exam preparation
- **Learning Outcomes:**
  - Master advanced competencies
  - Prepare for certification exams
  - Develop confidence for clinic readiness
- **Assessment:** Comprehensive final exam
